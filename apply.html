Safe and Clean, Sober Living
As part of Sanus House you will be required to live a lifestyle that promotes sober living. You will have obligations and expectations. It is expected you will attend a 12 step program and remain drug free. You will be required to take random UA tests. Most importantly, your particpation is voluntary, you may leave at any time. For a full set of the rules and expectations visit this page.
  • For safety reasons and lack of facilites, Sanus House is currently male only.
  • Sanus House does not discriminate for ANY reason. Your application is accepted or rejected on the basis of availablity and fit with the current residents.
  • We do ask your race and age strictly for the purpose of tracking an reporting of resident demographics. You are not required to answer these questions.
