Safe and Clean, Sober Living
As part of Sanus House you will be required to live a lifestyle that promotes sober living. You will have obligations and expectations. It is expected you will attend a 12 step program and remain drug free. You will be required to take random UA tests. Most importantly, your particpation is voluntary, you may leave at any time.
  • Sanus House is a male only facility.
  • Sanus House does not discriminate for ANY reason. Your application is accepted or rejected on the basis of availablity and fit with the current residents.
  • We ask demographic (race, religion, gender, age) questions strictly for the purpose of general analysis and reporting. You are not required to answer these questions and they have no bearing on your approval.
