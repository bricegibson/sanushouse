Welcome to Sanus House

Life is full of challenges and each of us choose our own way of coping with things we find hard and difficult. The directors here at Sanus House are no different than you. We have all struggled to manage our lives at one time or another. The vison of Sanus House is to help each of us find peace, hope and strenght through community.

Clean, Safe Comfortable Living

  • A community (the House) where we all work together and for each other.
  • Order and accountability. You will be accountable to yourself and the House.
  • Self-managed. The House sets the guidelines to follow. Each person has a voice.
  • With actively engaged directors and a House President, help and assistance is never far.
  • You will contribute your time and talents.
  • You will learn better habits and skills to manage the challenges life throws at you.

    Apply For Residency

    No two applicants are the same. No two life experiences are the same. When reviewing applicants we look for hope, determination, honesty and respect. We also look for fit to prevent conflict and provide a safe and rewarding environment.

    As an accepted applicant (resident) you are expected to participate in chores, keep a tidy living space, be respectful, be honest with others and yourself. You will attend a 12 step program of your choosing and submit to random UA tests. You will abide by a curfew. You will learn new skills along the way.

    Most rooms are shared with one and sometimes two other people. There are a few single rooms reserved for the House President and House Treasurer. Leadership roles are offered as the become available and are awarded based largely on commmitment and trust.

  • Monthly Room Rates:
  • Single - $600
  • Shared - $500
  • Deposit - $300 with $100 refundable when giving at least 30 day notice.
  • House President - $300 room discount with option for single room.
  • House Treasurer - $100 room discount with option for single room.

    • When paid on time, $10 of each rental payment is given back to the House to be used as the House chooses.

    How We Work

    Sanus House is about your recovery. It is our goal to build a community of support, strength and courage to overcome the things that destroy us and eat away at our souls. We aren't here to tell you what to do and when to do it. We belive in and promote ownership and self care. You own your recovery. We aren't here to force anyone into recovery. We know recovery only happens when the individual has a burning desire to change. It's a desire that is stronger than the desire to hide in the shadows of darkness.
