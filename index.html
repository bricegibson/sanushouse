Safe and Clean, Sober Living
Life is full of challenges and each of us choose our own ways of coping with things we find hard and difficult. The directors here at Sanus House are no differnt than you. We have all struggled to manage our lives. The vison of Sanus House is to help you find peace, hope and strenght through community.
At Sanus House we offer:
  • Clean, safe and comfortable living.
  • A community (the House) where we all work together and for each other.
  • Order and accountability. You will be accountable to yourself and the House.
  • Self-managed. The House sets the guidelines to follow. Each person has a voice.
  • Actively engaged directors and House President. Help and assistance is never far.
  • You will be expected to contribute your time and talents.
  • You will have the opportunity to learn better habits and skills to manage the challenges life throws at you.
