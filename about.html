About

Brice Gibson

Co-Founder, Sanus House

Brice has a BS degree in IT Management, is a Certified Project Management Professional and Licensed Real Estate Agent. His past career includes leadership roles as a Director, Vice-President and owner of several companies, largely under the brand of iKore. Brice builds exceptional teams, helping to grow the skills and talents of others. At Sanus House, he drives the vision and growth of the company.

Brice has a passion for living life in the outdoors and traveling far and wide. His greatest joy in life is his wife, children and grandchild. Brice is honored to be a part of Sanus House and to experience the lives changed through recovery.

Joann Gibson

Co-Founder, Sanus House

Joann has a Master’s in Education Leadership from Brigham Young University with 20 years’ experience as an educator and leader. Joann has raised two children and has always had an open door to friends, family, and the occasional stranger during their time of need. Joann is currently the caretaker for the greatest grandson in the world.

She feels fortunate to participate in making her brother's dream of helping others come true and looks forward to seeing the benefit to those on the road to recovery and to the communities they live in.

Gary Smith

Co-Founder, Sanus House

Gary obtained his GED and High School diploma while in prison in 2006. He has worked as a pipefitter, in residential construction, and as a heavy equipment operator. Gary looks forward to buying and remolding homes into sober living homes as we grow Sanus House.

Gary is passionate about treating others with respect, leading by example, and reaching out to those who have struggled with addiction. He is excited to give back in a meaningful way through Sanus House.

His personal goals include developing positive relationships, being a listening ear and showing himself and others there is hope in recovery.

Charlie Nelson

Co-Founder, Sanus House

Charlie is a High School graduate with a year of college and has worked 17 years in the carpentry business. His goals today include obtaining his contractor’s license and building future sober living homes.

Charlie is passionate about helping others discover a better life through sobriety. Having struggled with addiction, the consequences, and the recovery steps, Charlie is now able to help others address issues and difficult situations in a way that creates a positive outcome.

Charlie enjoys may outdoor activities and feeling of freedom of riding motorcycles.
